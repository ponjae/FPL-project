The Story of REAL-FPL

Football has been my greatest interest for as long as I can remember. I have been playing a lot of it myself but since I started my computer science degree the real ambition for becoming a professional player ended. However, another interest started. During my studies I discovered the world of Fantasy Premier League. How much fun it was and how it helped me enjoy football even more. My best friend from the university does also enjoy football (even though he supports Spurs...) and since the 2018/2019 season we have been competing against each other in the famous coffee of the week h2h-league as well as in the Port-a-Fort-league.

As our knowledge about the subject we studied grew we also became more and more interested in finding ways of leveraging our collected knowledge to improve our FPL-game. Last year we started experimenting and invented Botlas, a team that was using only actual data to find the best team. It worked quite well, but both he and I managed to beat it but I guess it was since we are such great managers ourselves... This year I wanted to take this idea to the next level and this is how this site was born. I wanted to present the "best" team based on data and according to the budget available. I know it is not optimal and can definitely be improved further but I think it can be used to give you some inspiration about your team. I used this link a lot when I figured out how to calculate the best possible team and this link is an excellent starting point when working with the api.

I hope you enjoy this site and feel free to reach out to me if you have any ideas how to improve it! / Punnmaestro
